About Us


We Are Independent

Truth is important to us. We ensure all reviews are unfiltered assessments by real customers making real transactions. With zero funding from the auto industry, we face no pressure to blow the whistle on any dealership.

We Are Transparent

Trust is important to us. Hence, we've made it possible for you to audit anything at AutoRated. Our salaries are published, our codebase is open-source, our meetings are live-streamed.

We Are Data-Driven

Completeness is important to us. Our AI-powered systems automatically identify new dealerships, new car makes and models, and update our repository to give you the most updated information.
